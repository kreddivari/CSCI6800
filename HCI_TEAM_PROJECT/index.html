Understanding the Problem

Problem Overview

Over a billion people around the world are suffering uncorrected refractory errors and blurred eyesight requiring glasses. Vision check or vision test process became very time taking with the doctors appointment and uncovered health insurance. With the huge demand for cost efficient and time saving process need to be invented. The Smart vision check,optical vending machine we design is capable of providing a detailed visual profile to your eye. not only it can save lot of time for people who need vision check,but also makes the whole process instantly.

User Characteristics

All users of this machine will presumably have a English reading and listening skills.The computer experience of our users will range from basic to advanced.The users could also have any level of education. The age of the users would range from older children to retirement age adults. However, people suffering uncorrected refractory errors are the young adult demographic. Geographically, our users can be located at the machine.Service assistance option is provided to the users who has disabilities on vision and who has no knowledge on the computer process.Service assistance can be request by the user based on clicking the help icon provided on the screen.

Task Analysis: Task Characteristics

The user will use this process for vision check which will be profiled by vision experiments and tests by the machine with the actions performed by the user.User will be provided by the microphone with high quality headphones to follow the instrctions by the machine and respond to the machine instractions in the process

Task Analysis: Task Environment

Task environment is very perticuar pertaining to the machine.The user will have to present in person at the machine where it is located. There are many possible locations where this machine may be used due to advances in technology. Thus, the environment where this task is performed is specific.The best suitable location for the machine is airport terminal lounges where users have enough wait time.To aware of the presence of the machine,there should be a guide process to attract the users to use the machine and to find it very clearly. The machine environment is a closed like telephone booth with no tansparent glass so that outside members cannot see inside users.The booth is intitially very lighted and clear to see the machine and it it will be very dark when starts the process. To give user experience more clear,Darker environment is made.The machine screen is wide large and have high resilution with large frames and icons to make user experience more feasible.The user will be instrsction by the voice commands and the touch screen options on the wide screen.The booth is made of no noise obsorption so that the user cannnot be effected by the outside noises.

An Analysis of the Existing Manual System

One of the most effective ways to check your vision profile without any scanning technology is through the manual process. We should find a local eye doctor to set up the vision checkup. According to the observation there are many people who is continuing the manual process which they feel is interactive and satisfactory. However, setting up doctor’s appointment and waiting many days to actually consult the doctor is time consuming. A typical process of manual process of any individual involves:

  • 1.) Request an appointment with local doctor.

  • 2.) waiting for the confirmation and consultation for many days usually.

  • 3.) setting up the pre-visit insurance formalities.

  • 4.) waiting for confirmation from insurance company.

  • 4.) Consulting the doctor.

  • 5.) Requesting appointment for eyeglasses.

  • 6.) visiting in person to select the eyeglasses according to the vision profile.

  • 7.) Requesting the follow-up appointment and consulting.

Strong Points and Deficiencies of the Existing Manual System

Automated vision check process is very quicker and time saving compared to the manual process. However, with the minor computer system errors in the automated process could raises the trustworthiness of the automated process. In the manual process, one can get the human interaction that could eliminates the doubts and nervousness of the patient where we can’t find in the automation process. Patient need to have a prior knowledge about computers and need to actively listen for the computer instructions. As some of the patients already have vision difficulties, it is difficult to see the screen and follow the computer instructions. As computer need to save all the vision profiles of many users, it is difficult to maintain the data and there are security concerns in various aspects. However, the manual process is time consuming it is very interactive. One can be clear of any doubts instantly and as discussing about their health issues regarding vision, they could get immense satisfaction.

The Larger Social and Technical System in Which Our Design Will Intersect

As the population getting increased rapidly worldwide, there is enormous insufficiency in the medical facilities. Automating a health checking process could save enormous resources and peoples time. Now a day working people are very busy with the everyday life and could have time to double on their health checkups and reports and intolerance of waiting time for doctor’s appointment, people are very interested about instant process. Automating the vision check could save a lot of time and very cost efficient. People who don’t have full coverage of vision insurance plans can also use the automatic vision check and save their time and money. The results are very accurate and instant, People can get sync with everyday life without major time consuming in their every day life.

Initial List of Usability Criteria

The usability of a product is affected not only by the features of the product itself, but also by the characteristics of the users, the tasks they are carrying out, and the technical, organizational and physical environment in which the product is used. One of the strongest advantages of our interface design is its simplicity, visibility and feasibility of the process. This product is intended for friendly conversation. The high-resolution touch screen monitor allows very clear and accurate on rendering the user interface. The user interface of this product contains a virtual keyboard which is visible on the screen, Menus, widgets, submit buttons in a very clear and contrast manner. The product is designed such a way to eliminate the hardware as much as possible. For example, introducing of virtual key board instead of keyboard hardware. The product is very feasible at the user interface such a way that user can choose the clear view of theme and contrast among many several themes. User interface has option to maximize the sizes of the interface in such a way that it allows the maximum zoom of 200%. The screen adjusts the interface based on the zoom level. The product interface is designed based on the design standards to avoid poor mappings and unwanted interface to avoid confusion to the user. Our user interface provides forward and backward options to the process to make user to skip some of the features and repeat the process if the user missed something on the process. The application provides a start on virtual tour for the first-time users to make sure the users will be knowledge enough before they start using the application.

Discussion of the Implications of What We Learned

As we expected, nearly all of the people with different age ranges we surveyed going in person to the vision check center after waiting many days for appointment and consultation.They expressed their condition is further worsen due to the time consuming for appointments and pre formalities of insurance corporations.All users expressed enthusiasm for the concept and possible prototype. They were comfortable with the advanced user interface and understood the suite of possible functionality offered to them. All users were able to follow the computer instructions and could easily go through the automated process.

Design Alternatives

Project Description

Smart vision check, optical vending machine is capable of not only providing a detailed visual profile your eye, but also vends the eyeglasses based on your vision profile. It can even detect the differences in how your eye sees during daylight and nighttime. It is loaded with powerful features that supports global standards in visual inspection. It also has digital image processing techniques that are in built for the facial scanning. It provides you a wide range of options to choose your type and style of eyeglasses, you can virtually choose your eyeglasses on the screen with your facial scanned image along with the best eyeglasses that suits to you.

Requirement Summary

Since the chosen locations for the kiosk are airports the care should be taken that the location is easily found. 1. Since some airports hold many business centers, the kiosk should be attractive and eye catchy that should easily be noticed by the customers. 2. An associate may be there to help the needy. 3. The effective explanation of the kiosk should be provided. 4. The average time taken for operating the kiosk is 7-10 min.

Design Space

The design space depends on the size, shape and occupancy of the smart machine. The operating method for the smart check kiosk should be easy for the user. The kiosk should provide easy and powerful query to search in a single entry, which facilitates users feel comfortable.

Design Summary

We have decided to provide easy and simple interface to the user to make the process easier and faster.The interface is desined in compatible with kiosk standard size screens with touch enabled.The whole process consistes of 3 phases with an quick start guide to first time users.The phase 1 is informative and user understadable.Phase 2 is oreover consists of machine instructions.Phase 3 consists of ending process with payment precosseing information.Apart from all the phases a common help icon is present in every interactive screnn so that user can get assistance at any point of time and phase.
